Matt Mccoy

J. Kevin Stophel is a member of the Stophel family that has been providing expert legal, tax and financial advice to the community for over 40 years. Kevin transitioned from a career at a Fortune 50 company to Merrill Lynch in the late 90’s as he wanted to apply his education more specifically to his career.

Kevin has a B.A. degree in Economics and a Masters degree in Personal Financial Planning. Additionally, he is a CERTIFIED FINANCIAL PLANNER® certificant, a Chartered Retirement Plans Specialist® and a Certified Investment Management Analyst®. Aside from helping his clients accomplish their goals, he has also been a branch manager for Merrill Lynch as well as a corporate trainer for new brokers. While at Merrill Lynch, he twice received the honor of Broker of the Year for the Tennessee Valley Complex. Before separating to found Kumquat, Kevin was a Vice President at Bank of America Merrill Lynch. Kevin brings a unique set of skills to the table and provides an innovative perspective while delivering expert, integrated solutions for his clients.

Kevin believes in his community and is actively a part of it volunteering significant amounts of his time to it. Aside from civic involvement in various non-profits in the city at both the board and volunteer levels, he coaches middle and elementary school soccer at Silverdale Baptist Academy. He is a member of the UTC College of Business Advisory Board. He is also active in the Downtown Rotary Club and supports its role in the community.

He is blessed to have Nikki as his wife and also three children, Jake, Christian and Elizabeth. He continues to play soccer and has a passion for financial research, apologetics, and marine aquariums.

Matt is an experienced financial advisor who has helped his clients accomplish their financial goals for over seven years. He earned a B.S. degree in Economics. In addition, he has been certified by the College for Financial Planning as a CERTIFIED FINANCIAL PLANNER® certificant, which focuses on comprehensive financial planning. Matt also holds the Chartered Financial Consultant (ChFC®) designation earned through The American College which focuses on the many uses of life insurance for financial and estate planning.

He is committed to a different way of thinking that doesn’t start with product but with process. His primary focus is on holistic financial planning tied to regular interaction with clients and their other core advisors. He is able to address each client’s unique financial situation through a detailed, risk focused approach. This process, coupled with his education and experience, provides the foundation for him to deliver solid solutions for clients.

Matt resides in Hixson, TN with his lovely wife Melanie and stepson Justin. He is an avid sports fan with a special affinity for baseball. He is also a student of his industry and enjoys financial reading and research.
